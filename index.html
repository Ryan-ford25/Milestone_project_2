Echoes of light

Rules

The rules for this game are simple.

  • Click the button below to start the game.
  • The squares within the grid will then begin to light up in a random sequence.
  • Your task is to memorize the sequence shown to you and relay that exact sequence by clicking on the relevent squares.
  • In this version of the game you have three lives, if you fail to repeat the sequence a life will be lost.
  • If you succeed then the game will progress and another sequence will be shown.
